Client Application Maintenance Document FAQ
Andrew Patterson, Alex Bruce & Zahava Alston

What devices does this app run on?
Duke CPR is designed primarily for iPhones; however, the app can run in “stretched-mode” on iPads.  All versions of iPhone and iPad will work with this app, assuming that they are running a supported
version of iOS (see below).

What operating systems does this app support?
Duke CPR works on iOS 5 and above.

Does the app need access to the Internet?
Crisis Mode and CPR training mode are fully functional without access to the Internet.  With a few exceptions, the “learn more” section needs access to the Internet.

How much space does this app use?
The app takes up less than 100 MB.
